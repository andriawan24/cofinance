## Design Context

### Users
Cofinance is for personal users who want to take care of their money with less stress. The profile area should help them manage identity details and monthly finance-cycle settings quickly, clearly, and without noise.

### Brand Personality
Simple, calm, trustworthy.

The interface should feel reassuring and steady rather than flashy or gamified. It should support quick routine maintenance without making money management feel intimidating.

### Aesthetic Direction
Reference direction: Money Manager.

Use a calm, flagship-quality product UI with rounded surfaces, soft contrast, clean hierarchy, and restrained accent color. Support both light and dark themes. Avoid crypto-dashboard energy, neon accents, loud gradients, glassmorphism, and decorative clutter.

### Design Principles
1. Clarity over decoration: every element should support a clear task or state.
2. Calm confidence: use soft surfaces, measured contrast, and quiet emphasis.
3. One system, not one-offs: spacing, shapes, and interaction patterns should stay consistent across profile and edit-profile flows.
4. Touch-first polish: controls should feel comfortable on phones and remain readable on larger screens.
5. Trust through restraint: no flashy effects, no ambiguous actions, no overloaded layouts.
